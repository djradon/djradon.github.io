Maple Serum on KZSU 90.1 FM

updated: 29 april 1996

Maple Serum is the name of my radio show. I play a variety of musical styles that could be roughly categorized as college-radio-type stuff, shoving Canadian content in wherever I can.

A cool online music preferences database is firefly at MIT's Media Lab. It uses AI-type techniques to recommend music.

Check out the Radio-Resitor's Bulletin at http://www.rootsworld.com/rw/feature/rrb.html

If you're a member of the Stanford community, get involved with the station. You'll meet some of the most interesting people on campus.
