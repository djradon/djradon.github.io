Station Information

KZSU Radio is Stanford's all-volunteer, student-run radio station, broadcasting at 90.1FM in the San Francisco Bay Region. Our signal reaches Oakland in the north, Fremont in the east, San Jose in the south, and select locations throughout the city of San Francisco.
